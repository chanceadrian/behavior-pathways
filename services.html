Coaching Services
Effective instruction and behavior management.
As an experienced educator and certified coach, I offer personalized services catering to the unique needs of teachers, administrators, and those early in their careers. Choose from a range of options designed to enhance instruction and behavior management.
For Teachers
Classroom Observation & Feedback
By spending time directly observing you in action, I gain invaluable context about strengths, gaps, and dynamics with different students. My non-evaluative feedback offers objective insights tailored to improving specific on-the-ground challenges.
School Consultation
I’ve worked as a full time BCBA in four school districts in Texas and have provided consultation services to many other school districts. My school consultation services include:

  1. Development of ABA-based classrooms in schools
  2. Classroom consultation and coaching in special and general education
  3. Crisis management Training (QBS Safety Care Trainer)
  4. Functional Behavior Assessment and Behavior Intervention Plan development in schools
  5. Large and small group staff development
  6. Severe problem behaviors in individuals of all ages

For Administrators
Workshops & Talks
I deliver interactive sessions at schools and conferences to inspire educators with frameworks and techniques to elevate their practice. Blending models like PBIS and RTI, I equip attendees to return to classrooms with renewed passion, self-awareness and tangible skills to better serve students.

For New Grads
Mentorship & Supervision
I also provide BACB supervision for those pursuing certification in behavior analysis and mentorship for those early in their careers.
BCBA/BCaBA Supervision
I have provided supervision to over 25 individuals pursuing certification as BCBA’s throughout my career. Many of these professionals have been successfully employed as BCBA’s in the field with various organizations, and some have opened and maintained their own ABA practices. My specializations include the following areas:

  1. School based ABA services
  2. Functional Behavior Assessment and Behavior Intervention
  3. Severe problem behaviors in individuals of all ages
  4. Adolescent and adult ABA services
  5. Clinical supervision
