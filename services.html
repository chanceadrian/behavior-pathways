Coaching Services
Behavioral
management that works.
As an experienced educator and certified coach, I offer personalized, research-based coaching and professional development services tailored to enhance instruction, behavior management, and career growth for teachers, administrators, and education professionals.
For Teachers
Classroom Observation & Feedback
By spending time directly observing you in action, I gain invaluable context about strengths, gaps, and dynamics with different students. My non-evaluative feedback offers objective insights tailored to improving specific on-the-ground challenges.
School Consultation
I’ve worked as a full time BCBA in four school districts in Texas and have provided consultation services to many other school districts. My school consultation services include:

  1. Development of ABA-based classrooms in schools
  2. Classroom consultation and coaching in special and general education
  3. Crisis management Training (QBS Safety Care Trainer)
  4. Functional Behavior Assessment and Behavior Intervention Plan development in schools
  5. Large and small group staff development
  6. Severe problem behaviors in individuals of all ages

Overview
What to expect
Collaborative goal setting.
Together we identify priority areas for improvement and establish clear, measurable goals that address your specific classroom needs.
I check in regularly to monitor and adjust goals as needed.
Classroom observation & feedback.
I will observe your teaching and class dynamics firsthand, taking careful notes about interactions, strengths, gaps, and areas of opportunity.
Post-observation, I provide insight based on my expertise and collaborate with you to pinpoint next steps.
Targeted strategy recommendations.
Drawing from proven, research-backed techniques, I suggest tailored interventions across academic instruction, classroom/behavior management, social-emotional learning, and more to suit each unique situation.
Ongoing support & mentoring.
Over scheduled calls and messages, I offer consistent availability to answer questions, problem-solve challenges, provide materials, celebrate wins, and keep you focused on established goals for continuous improvement.
